Research Line

Informed Machine Learning with Knowledge Landmarks

This page tracks the knowledge-landmarks line, where sparse trusted anchors or landmark structures may guide prediction, representation, or regularization without requiring a full symbolic rule system.

Research significance

Knowledge landmarks may provide a middle ground between hard logical supervision and fully unconstrained learning. For the current website, this line is useful as a softer structural-prior route that can later be compared with logic rules, semantic losses, and granular descriptions.
